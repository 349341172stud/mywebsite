Favourite Musician

Mac DeMarco

Why I Like His Music

Mac DeMarco is simply an amazing artist; that’s very subjective. I believe the question of what truly makes an artist so great is how their art can speak to the soul of an individual and to their unintended audience. All his songs speak to me on a personal level, and his songs evoke emotion through the sometimes melancholic harmony of his carefully crafted lyrics and heavenly melody.

I believe that, without a doubt, art is the extension of an artist’s soul. Mac DeMarco, as a person, is soft-spoken, care-free, and very creatively expressive. It is as if the universe spoke to him spiritually. To DeMarco, music is more than an expression of his soul—it’s a method to cope with reality. I believe it was Giacomo Leopardi who said that nature itself was indifferent to human suffering, and that happiness was structurally, permanently out of reach. Mac DeMarco, on a personal level, understands this worldview and uses music as a way to cope. His journey is not one of a simple human being, but of a poet and an eminent scholar in human nature.
