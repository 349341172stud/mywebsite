Hobbies

I have quite a few hobbies, being martial arts, writing, reading, and programming. Within martial arts, I have ranked third place twice in a regional karate tournament, and I have done boxing for 9 months. I have recently started my BJJ journey, and I hope to get stronger and meet new people. Writing is a big passion of mine, and it intertwines with reading. I get a lot of inspiration from the novels I read, such as Reverend Insanity. I have already written one novel, which I have not published, since it was a gift made for a close friend of mine. The word count was around 42,000, and I completed it in around one week after a few months of planning. I stayed up until 6 A.M. on the deadline, December 22nd, and I can say that it was worth all the elbow grease I put into it. I am learning how to code in Roblox, and previously I released one Minecraft mod on the popular modding website CurseForge.

Strengths

My single greatest strength is perseverance. I have other strengths, however, I felt that the most important strength to talk about would be perseverance since it is the foundation for all my other strengths. Without perseverance, there would be no point in having hope, having intelligence, or having physical prowess. The only option and choice I have is perseverance, and perseverance has become an essential part of my character. It is by far my greatest strength, and without it, none of my other strengths would matter in the slightest.

Character

I am a person who is inherently contradictory. I care about objectivity, facts, logical argumentation, and proof; I care about poetry, art, fiction, literature, and expression. I am a person. I’m a human being. I’m flawed in many ways, and interests often conflict with one another. However, I am a true person. I will stick to my beliefs no matter what, and I will never consciously go against my values unless the situation warrants it. That’s who I am.
